ELDEN RING

About this Game

Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring and become an Elden Lord in the Lands Between. A vast world where open fields with a variety of situations and huge dungeons with complex and three-dimensional designs are seamlessly connected. As you explore, the joy of discovering unknown and overwhelming threats await you, leading to a high sense of accomplishment. A multilayered story told in fragments. An epic drama in which the various thoughts of the characters intersect in the Lands Between.
Genres RPG, Adventure, Action
Features Open World, Multiplayer
