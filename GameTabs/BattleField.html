Battle Field 2042

About this Game

Battlefield™ 2042 brings back the iconic all-out warfare of the franchise. Engage in intense, immersive multiplayer battles using cutting-edge weapons. Season 7: Turning Point takes the fight to the Atacama Desert in Chile, featuring lawless combat on new map Haven and fan-favorite Stadium. Utilize new gear like the SCZ-3 SMG and Predator SRAW gadget. Unlock 100 tiers of Battle Pass content for ultimate power. Experience large-scale warfare and close-quarters combat on maps from 2042 and classic titles. Discover unexpected battles from Battlefield's past and present in Battlefield Portal.
